About LaRuta

With over 15 years of experience teaching English, Spanish, and Portuguese, and as a Japanese language learner myself, I realized I had all the tools necessary to help students study remotely.

Having lived abroad, I understand firsthand the challenges and stakes involved in communicating effectively. Whether it’s expressing ideas clearly or overcoming language barriers, I’ve been there. My time in Japan, where I developed a basic vocabulary and mastered essential language structures, taught me invaluable lessons.
Similarly, living in Brazil for nearly four decades, I’ve come to appreciate the complexities of Portuguese pronunciation. Since my teenage years, when I struggled to communicate without unintentionally making people laugh, I’ve understood the importance of mastering the fundamental sounds of any language you set out to learn.

"La Ruta" is designed to help you build a strong foundation, enabling you to feel increasingly confident when expressing yourself. As we guide you through the process of enhancing your speaking skills, we’ll encourage you to take steady steps forward to achieve the goals that inspired you to start this course.
"La Ruta" will guide you on your journey and support you every step of the way.

Wishing you an excellent journey!
